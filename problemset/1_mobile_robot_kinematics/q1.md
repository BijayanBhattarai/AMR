# The 90 Degree Swedish Wheel

## Degrees of Freedom

How many degrees of freedom does this wheel have?

Answer: 3 

- Rotation around the contact point point 
- Y axis motion by wheel rotation
- X axis motion by Swedish wheel rotation

## The Wheel Equation

Answer: 

- j1 = sin(alpha + beta)
- j2 = -cos(alpha + beta)
- j3 = -l * cos(beta)
